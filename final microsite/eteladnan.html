Etel Adnan
Etel Adnan (Arabic: إيتيل عدنان‎; born 24 February 1925 in Beirut, Lebanon) is a Lebanese-American poet, essayist, and visual artist.

Besides her literary output, Adnan continues to produce visual works in a variety of media, such as oil paintings, films and tapestries, which have been exhibited at galleries across the world. She lives in Paris and Sausalito, California.
